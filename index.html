About us

We are Grassroots and have arisen from the sisters of Portland , Beaverton, and surrounding areas. Many sisters at the core of Muslimahs United were connected prior to the formation of Muslimahs United through the masjid and the greater community.

We’ve been a group that Muslims sisters can turn to when other organizations or agencies can’t or are no longer able to help.
